Introduction

Building your dream home is an exciting journey that starts with a thoughtful home design plan. Your home is where you create memories, so it’s essential to have a house design that balances comfort, functionality, and a touch of personalisation.

In this article, we’ll guide you through 10 essential spaces that will transform your house into the home of your dreams.

Comfortable bedrooms

Your bedroom is the heart of your home, making it the most important part of any home design plan. Ensure it is well-lit and ventilated, with plenty of storage like built-in wardrobes or under-bed drawers to accommodate big trunks, baby care products, or pooja items. Consider adding a cosy reading nook or a small workspace. Don’t forget to install blackout curtains to control light and noise for a restful environment.

For children or elderly parents:

  • Children: Use childproof furniture, soft flooring, and storage for toys and study space.
  • Elderly: Prioritize accessibility with non-slip flooring, easy storage, and ground-floor placement.

VASTU TIP :

Place the study in the west or southwest to enhance focus and stability.
