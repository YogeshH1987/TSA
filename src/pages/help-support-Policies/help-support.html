Order cancellation policy

Pay now options

The amount will be refunded to the customer’s bank account (used for the payment) after deducting standard payment gateway transaction charges.

Cancellation requests made after 24 hours or after dispatch will be rejected, and the customer must take the material.

If the cancellation request is accepted, the refund will be processed within 10 working days.

Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of placing the order or before dispatch, whichever comes first.

Pay before dispatch

Orders can be canceled by the customer through the portal or toll-free number before the order is dispatched from the dealer’s outlet.

Cancellation requests made after dispatch will be rejected, and the customer must take the material.
